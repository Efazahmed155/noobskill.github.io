TRAFFIC JAM

Traffic jam is a long line of vehicles that can not move or that can only move very slowy because there is much traffic on the road. It is a common affair in big cities and towns in our courtry. It is one of the major problem. The result of traffic jam is untold. The density of the population is the main couse of traffic jam. The increase in the number of busses, trucks, autorickshaw, rickshaws, and innapropriate traffic control system is all so responsible for it.
